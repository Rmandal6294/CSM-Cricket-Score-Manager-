Cricket Score Manager

A modern, intuitive cricket scoring solution designed for clubs, schools, and cricket enthusiasts.

Comprehensive Scoring

Track every run, wicket, and over with precision. CSM handles all the complexities of cricket scoring so you can focus on the game.

Team Management

Create and manage custom teams with detailed player profiles, roles, and statistics that persist across matches.

Mobile Responsive

Score matches from anywhere using any device. CSM works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

No Installation Required

Access CSM directly from your browser without downloading or installing any software. Start scoring in seconds.

Core Features

  • Team Management: Create and customize teams with detailed player profiles including names, jersey numbers, and specialized roles.
  • Authentic Toss Simulation: Start your match with a realistic toss that determines which team bats or bowls first.
  • Real-time Scoring: Record runs, wickets, extras, and other events with a simple, intuitive interface that updates instantly.
  • In-depth Statistics: Track run rates, boundaries, partnerships, and other critical metrics automatically.
  • Ball-by-Ball Analysis: Review every delivery with detailed ball-by-ball breakdowns and commentary.
  • Multi-device Support: Score matches from any device with a responsive design that adapts to your screen size.
